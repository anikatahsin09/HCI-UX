Reverse UX: Purposefully Frustrating Interfaces

Welcome to our research prototype. This project explores the fascinating world of **human-computer interaction** by doing the opposite of what's expected. We've designed an interface that is deliberately confusing, inconsistent, and difficult to use. Our goal is to observe how you, the user, navigate and recover from these purposeful frustrations. Please continue to the prototype to begin the experience.
